## Announcement of price update for IOT(Inter-operability Testing) at HEMS (ECHONET Lite) Certification Support Center

We will update the price for IOT(Inter-operability Testing) at HEMS (ECHONET Lite) Certification Support Center on June 1st 2026.

### 1. Abstract

Current price structure is basic service(the fee for the usage of equipment) and optional service(technical support by the stuff). New price structure will be the fee for the usage of equipment include technical support.

### 2. The fee

You can select half day (AM 9:30 – 13:15,　PM 13:15 – 17:00) or full day (9:30 - 17:00) usage.
