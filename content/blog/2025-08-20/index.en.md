On Wednesday, August 20, 2025, Mr. Xie Qunxiang from Taiwan ETC (*1) and Professor Hu Zhilin from National Central University (Taoyuan City, Taiwan) visited the HEMS Certification Support Center.

Taiwan ETC is a government-affiliated third-party testing and certification organization in Taiwan. It conducts testing and certification for CNS 16014 (TaiSEIA 101), a standard communication protocol for smart home devices promoted in Taiwan, and is also engaged in research on interoperability with “ECHONET Lite (ISO/IEC 14543-4-3),” an international standard communication protocol for IoT widely used in Japan.

With National Central University, we signed a mutual cooperation and collaboration agreement (*2) in October 2024 to promote and support a joint research project on energy management systems. Through this collaboration, we are advancing research on the application of ECHONET Lite technology in Taiwan.

During the visit, in addition to a courtesy call on President Inoue of our university, technical discussions were held on protocol interoperability between TaiSEIA and ECHONET Lite. Regarding this interoperability, the Smart House Research Center is currently developing a protocol bridge to enable integration, thereby promoting the use of ECHONET Lite-compatible devices in energy management systems in Taiwan. Furthermore, National Central University introduced a student-developed TaiSEIA control application, followed by discussions on future collaboration related to ECHONET Lite integration.

The Smart House Research Center will continue to promote international research and development on the utilization of ECHONET Lite technologies.

(※1) Taiwan ETC：Electronics Testing Center
