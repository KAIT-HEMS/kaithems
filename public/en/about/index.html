Inter-Operability Testing

Various ECHONET Lite–compatible devices are installed within the Smart House Research Center to build an interoperability testing environment. This environment is used not only for in-house research but is also made available to external users. In particular, the room equipped with smart electricity meters from electric power companies across Japan constitutes a unique testing environment within the country.
